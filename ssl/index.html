Steps on how to configure your https connection.

Visit the following article for more information and specific steps for other operating systems than OSx.

Step 0

This should be the first time you setup up the application. A pop-message appeared, to let you know that your administrator's rights are required to create a self-signed certificate. You were prompted your administrator password which was used to generate a personal certificate to use to transfer data securely. Herein, we will ask the web browser to accept this certificate.

Step 1

In a new tab, open the following page it should display a privacy error. The certificate generated is not yet accepted by the browser.

Step 2

Click on the icon next to the URL.

Step 3

Click on the Details link

Step 4

Click on View Certificate. A small window with the certificate icon will apear.

Step 5

Drag the certificate icon onto your desktop.

Step 6

Double-click on the certificate. This will add it to you keychain as well as launch the keychain tool.

Step 7

In the keychain tool, double-click on the connector.plot.ly certificate to open the settings for that certificate. Click on the Trust dropdown and select the 'Always trust' in the 'When Using This Certificate' dropdown.
